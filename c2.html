Family Counselling

What types of your family problems can counselling help?

  • Problems regard to communication
  • Economic hardships
  • Anger problems
  • Disruption as a result of separation or divorce
  • Problems with stepfamily life or when a new partner moves in
  • Problem behaviour with adolescents or older children
  • Children leaving home
  • Unwanted pregnancy
  • Changes in family structure (result of a bereavement or illness)
  • Parent/child conflict
  • Violence, abuse, and gaslighting
  • Conflict around culture, religion, or lifestyle
  • Addiction to alcohol / other drugs
    • Not only about your problems, if you want to talk with us and relax your mind, we can listen to you.
